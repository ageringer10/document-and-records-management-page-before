Document and Records Management

About the Service

ServiceSource has extensive experience in document management. Our document handling procedures have quality assurance measures built into each step. At every phase of the process, from document preparation, through imaging, to document re-assembly, a strict regimen is enforced to ensure accuracy and security of your documents.

ServiceSource has hands-on experience in performing complex document management programs that require precision imaging; extensive capture and classification; technical development and integration and comprehensive quality control procedures.
